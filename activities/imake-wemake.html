IMake WeMake Competition

One day in summer, my mom asked me to partake in this competition. She kept nagging me about it, and I was like "yeah, yeah, I'll get to it."

Then one day during a Tech class, Sir Cy introduced the same competition for us to participate in. Needless to say, the opportunity was there and I took it.

Sir Cy paired me up with Led and Joanna to soon qualify for pitching our project (problem originally concieved by Sir JR) and subsequently developing it.

We were able to get something out of the contest; we now have an extra arduino (Leonardo), a Seeed shield, and a lot of wires and Grove modules. We also got an Acer Cloudprofessor from the competition, which is, in my personal opinion, pretty useless to us because of the unreliability of the internet here in our country.

We soon made it our Tech project and my Leadership SCALE activity. Took care of forms, materials, and we were set for the rest of the year.

Overall, it was a nice experience. I got to drink with a bunch of guys and girls from CARC and EVC and even pulled a smooth move with one of them after everything.
