Fitness Program

The hardest SCALE activity that I've done. The apps I used to guide me helped, though.

I learned from this activity that emotions, studies and mental stuff could actually affect me do physical stuff. Well, when I first met my ex I was really motivated to make myself look good for her, and was able to lose weight. Then, when stress came my way (from academics and other stuff), I kinda focused on comforting myself more than staying fit. When I broke up with my ex, I really let myself go and gained weight.

But now I know that I can lose weight, and I've given myself the mindset that losing fat isn't impossible after all. It just takes time.

I have been working out irregularly, so I feel like I have strong abs even though I have a fat belly.
